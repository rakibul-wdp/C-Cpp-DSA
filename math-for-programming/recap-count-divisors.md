### Floor, Ceiling, Round

- Floor: Nearest integer below
- Ceiling: Nearest integer above
- Round: Nearest integer
- 12.65 -> Floor: 12, Ceiling: 13, Round: 13
- 9.21 -> Floor: 9, Ceiling: 10, Round: 9
- 24 -> Floor: 24, Ceiling: 24, Round: 24
