# Series

- Sequence: List of numbers with some order or pattern
  - 1, 3, 5, 7, 7
  - 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, ...
- Series: Sum of elements in a sequence
  - S1 = 1 + 3 + 5 + 7 + 9
  - s2 = 1 + 1 + 2 + 3 + 5 + 8 + 13 + ...
- Finite vs Infinite series
- Arithmetic vs Geometric Series
  - 1 + 6 + 11 + 16 + 21 + ...
  - 2 + 6 + 18 + 54 + ...
